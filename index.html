I am a PhD Candidate studying Human Biology and Reproductive Ecology at Northwestern University. My research focuses on reproduction, physiology and epi/genetics, as well as development and inheritance.

I did my B.Sc.(Hons.) in Zoology at the University of Manitoba, and my M.Sc. in Biology at Simon Fraser University. I have also studied and done research at the University of Wollongong in Australia, the Roslin Institute (where they cloned Dolly, the sheep!) and the Center for Ecology and Hydrology just outside Edinburgh, Scotland.

My other passions are martial arts (currently Brazilian Jiujitsu, formerly Capoeira and boxing), motorcycles, and [deep electronic music] (https://soundcloud.com/aquatec).
